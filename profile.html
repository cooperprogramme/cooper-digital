Biography

Alistair Cooper has been providing design services for over 7 years to a host of global clients, from New York to Hong Kong. Collaborating directly with marketers, directors, audiences and developers (both internal and external).

Planning, designing and maintaining digital ownership through functional usability testing and accessibility compliancy. Managing communication and design assets from initial conception through to post-launch maintenance.

Guided by fundamental graphic principles and systematic design, with a ‘traditional’ design education. Cooper focuses on the essence of identity, communication, interaction and experience – initiating projects in a collaborative, agile approach to ensure clarity and consistency of message, maintaining industry standard methodologies and accessibility.
